COMET Services Needs For Schools And Students

The traditional education system in our country does not consider the career development aspect in its curriculum. Career counselling and development services are formally associated with very expensive schools in major cities.

As a result, career counselling related services are inaccessible to more than 90% of students who study in remote parts of the country. This prevents students from achieving their best. COMET is working to close this known gap by helping such schools and students who are deprived of career counselling services



Comet Services For Schools


The COMET Foundation has been supporting the schools with the following ongoing initiatives, which greatly adds to the schools' growing wall of fame.

  • Career counselling sessions/workshops: help in creating awareness in students regarding career development.

  • Website development and Digital Marketing training for class 12 students.

    • COMET is going to launch this initiative for all class 12 students who aspire to learn website development and digital marketing skills or wish to make a career in information technology.

    Scope of the initiative:

    • All registered students will be trained on website development tools like HTML, dHTML, CSS, JS, Word press and no-code/low-code technologies to make simple websites.

    • Students will also be trained on skills needed for Digital marketing.

    Benefits of this initiative:

    • Students will gain the most relevant skills for the present market, including website creation and digital marketing.

    • This initiative will greatly benefit students pursuing careers in the IT industry.

    • This initiative will be especially beneficial for those poor students who want to do internships along with their graduation course.

    • This initiative will provide students with the opportunity to create websites and engage in digital marketing to promote the activities of remote villagers. Thus, this has the potential to significantly increase self-employment and entrepreneurship in remote areas.


  • Opportunity to participate in various Inter-school competitions COMET conducts numerous competitions that schools can participate in, to foster healthy competition, creativity, teamwork, and skill development among students.

These not only provide opportunities for students to showcase their talents but also encourage teamwork, leadership, and personal growth.



Comet Preferred Schools


We invite schools from remote places to join the COMET preferred schools list. These are the schools who wish to get COMET services on a regular basis. Any school which wants to avail the COMET benefits can register itself as COMET preferred school by contacting the COMET management team.COMET preferred schools get the above mentioned COMET Services free of cost.

Procedure to register for COMET preferred schools list.

School management needs to send request to the COMET Change leaders in mail or in WhatsApp
