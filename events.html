Events

We believe that events are crucial for fostering an all-round development among students. We at COMET conduct the following categories of events on regular basis -

  • Technical workshops
  • Sports
  • Festivals and birthday celebrations

    • Academic events such as technical workshops promote intellectual growth, while cultural events like festivals celebrate diversity and creativity. Social events such as sports competitions help build friendships and teamwork skills, enhancing the overall experience.
