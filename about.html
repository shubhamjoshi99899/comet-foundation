ABOUT COMET

eCOMET Foundation, a non-profit organization and a group of 40+ IT senior management professionals/experts from different IT companies, serving a common social cause to help the underprivileged schools and students from remote places who are deprived of career development related opportunities, either due to geographical or financial constraints.

The above mentioned gap prohibits the students to achieve as per their best abilities. Hence, COMET works towards resolving these known gaps by enabling students to identify their potential skills and guide them closely to convert their potential skills into success stories



Our Belief

We have highly talented students in remote places who are not able to showcase their BEST due to lack of facilities and quality education. If such students are closely mentored in a disciplined environment, they can be transformed into BEST skilled professionals.



Working model

  1. COMET trainings are provided online combined with class-room mode via COMET Study centre.

  2. Sessions are delivered by IT experts from Hyderabad, Bangalore and other locations.

  3. Workshops are conducted during weekends in COMET Study Centre.
